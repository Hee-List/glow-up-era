GLOW UP ERA:

WHAT IS GLOW UP ERA ABOUT?

A glow up is meant to be a transformational period where people like to improve themselves in a holistic way focusing on all fields that they deem necessary to self improve. 

A glow up journey does not mean being trendy and being aesthetic it is about taking small steps to improve ones confidence and self worth gradually to create a better version of yourself that you can feel proud of.  

A glow up can vary depending on the person and is meant to hold a deep personal connection with the person and their values thus holding a different meaning with each individual. 

A glow up era is not to be used as a way of perfectionism but to create progress to grow and develop as a person. Whether there changes an individual seeks be gradual or dramatic during the process of a glow up you are bound to discover new things about yourself while feeling better in your own skin reminding oneself about the unlimited potential somebody has kept with them self.
