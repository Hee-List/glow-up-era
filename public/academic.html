What Is An Academic Glow Up?

An academic growth is the process of approaching one's intellect with the motive of improving their intellectual growth, approach to learning and academic success. It is normally used to refer to the shift in mindsets from simply wanting to “just pass” or “meet the requirements needed” to wanting to willingly improve or engage in their personal development thus excelling in future tasks

Often people blur the lines between being motivated or cultivating the discipline of wanting to study. Through the use of study techniques, better time management, or setting clear study goals to improve one's academic success all these things can help to achieve higher academic success. The shift in one's mindset is crucial as it helps to view things like challenges as opportunities to improve and learn instead of something to avoid and deflect. This is normally referred to as a growth mindset as it is not about being perfect but about discipline, consistency, hard work.

Accepting help can be challenging but it can help you gain new ways to improve yourself academically by clarifying context that was too hard for you to do alone. This can be done by reaching out to teachers, parents, friends, tutors etc.

Timed management is a skill that will help prioritise tasks that are important while helping you stay organised. This in the long run helps to reduce stress and anxiety from having to cram things in to finish on time this also helps to improve academic performances and engage a higher level of understanding of the things being studied.

By implementing study techniques like active recall, spaced repetition and feynman technique you can slowly improve your academic success by using structured techniques that work well for you.

Setting clear study goals can help you overcome distractions and stay focused because you have a clear understanding of what you want to accomplish. Which would help you achieve your goals quicker.
