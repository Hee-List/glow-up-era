What Is A Mental Glow Up?

A mental glow up refers to the cleansing of the brain, it's about the improvement of your mindset which helps to grow the inner strength and mental well being of the person. By welcoming a mental shift of the head you will slowly realise how important inner growth and mental health is for yourself as you embrace self awareness.

A mental glow up involves factors like imbedding self reflection, positivity, finding ways to comfort yourself through tough times to limit the amount of negative thoughts absorbed by your cognitive thinking. Activities like journaling, meditation or even reading is a good way to understand yourself as once you learn to understand yourself and your body you will know what your body needs which helps improve mental health.

By finding the balance between the emotions of oneself and the inner thoughts produced, you will be able to handle hurdles and challenges better without having to put yourself down through the process as this glow up helps to increase the confidence by reducing the stress and negative thoughts of a person through personal growth and a growth mindset.
