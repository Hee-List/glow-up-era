What Is A Lifestyle Glow Up?

Focuses on the day to day activities that shape the way an individual spends their time such as daily routines, habits and even the quality of one's life. This can be influenced by the environment you grow up in or even the people around you. This type of glow up does not focus on just one aspect but all of them combined to create a balanced and healthy lifestyle.
